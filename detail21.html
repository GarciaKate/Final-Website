Tormund Giantsbane

On his massive arms he wears golden bands engraved with runes of the First Men, which have been passed down by his forefathers. Tormund is armored with heavy ringmail taken from a dead ranger. The often jovial Tormund enjoys food and drink, especially ale and mead. He is styled Tall-talker, Horn-blower and Breaker of Ice, Husband to Bears, the Mead-king of Ruddy Hall, Speaker to Gods and Father of Hosts. Mance Rayder named him Horn-blower for the power of Tormund's lungs. It is said that Tormund can laugh the snow off mountaintops.He distrusts the men of the Frozen Shore.Tormund does not know how to read.

About

Who played the role?

Kristofer Hivju

A second generation Norwegian actor, Kristofer Hivju was born on 7 December 1978 in Oslo. His father Erik Hivju is a prominent character actor who appeared with his son in the short film Flax where Kristofer shared screenwriting credit with director Bård Ivar Engelsås. Kristofer made his American Film debut in Matthijs van Heijningen Jr.'s 2011 remake of John Carpenter's The Thing. Next he was seen in M. Night Shyamalan's After Earth but he is most recognizable as fan favorite Tormund Giantsbane on HBO's Game of Thrones.
