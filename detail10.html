Gendry

Gendry is a blacksmith apprentice for master Tobho Mott in King's Landing. When Gendry was old enough, an unknown lord paid his apprentice fee so he could learn to be a blacksmith; he does not know he is a bastard son of King Robert I. Gendry was born in King's Landing, and does not know who his father was. His mother was a worker at an alehouse, who died when he was still a young boy. All he remembers of her was that she had blond hair. Tobho Mott, a master smith, took him on as an apprentice after being paid double the customary fee by a man who concealed his identity.It is possible that this man was Varys, using one of his disguises.

Who played the role?

Joe Dempsie

Joe Dempsie was born on June 22, 1987 in Liverpool, Merseyside, England as Joseph Maxwell Dempsie. He is an actor, known for Skins (2007), Blitz (2011) and Game of Thrones (2011).
