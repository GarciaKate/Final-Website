Ellaria Sand

Although not accounted as a beautiful woman, Ellaria is regarded as attractive and eye-catching, with an exotic, sensuous flair. She has black hair. She is described as a good woman, strong and brave, with a gentle heart. Ellaria is believed to worship a Lysine love goddess,and is described by Oberyn Martell as being sexually adventurous and potentially bisexual.Angry at Oberyn for having crippled Willas Tyrell, Olenna Tyrell considers Ellaria to be "the serpent's whore".

About

Who played the role?

Indira Varma

Indira Varma was born on September 27, 1973 in Bath, Somerset, England. She is an actress and producer, known for Kama Sutra: A Tale of Love (1996), Exodus: Gods and Kings (2014) and Anna (2013).
