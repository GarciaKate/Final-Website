Greyworm

Grey Worm has absolutely no facial hair, though the hair atop his head is short and brown. He is stocky, of medium height and square. He has a serious looking face, on the edge of solemn. The Unsullied do not display much emotion. He is young, in his late twenties or so.

About

Who played the role?

Jacob Anderson

Jacob Anderson was born on June 18, 1990 in London, England. He is an actor, known for Game of Thrones (2011), Adulthood (2008) and Comedown (2012).
